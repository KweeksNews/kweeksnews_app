KweeksNews is a website designed as a medium to accommodate various content such as articles, news, short stories, videos, podcasts, and so on from various societies/elements such as student, student organisations, communities, teachers, and all societies/elements of Madrasah Mu'allimin Muhammadiyah Yogyakarta.

Now, KweeksNews is available in the form of an application to make it easier for users to access the latest posts directly from their mobile device!

<b>Features</b>

- Native app experiences
- In-app post viewer
- In-app comment viewer
- Search for posts
- Save posts offline
- Dark mode
